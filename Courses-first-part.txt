Course Structure for Computer Science & Coding - Course 1
This is Course 1 under the Computer Science & Coding Subject. The course contains 15
levels. All levels will initially remain locked except for Level 1. Users can unlock each
subsequent level upon completing the previous one, regardless of their quiz score.
Below is the step-by-step description of content placement and flow. Some technical
terms may appear in English where Urdu equivalents are not commonly used.
Developer notes have been included throughout to clarify implementation requirements.
The content will follow this structure:
First, the main lesson text will be displayed
This will be followed by relevant examples
Each section will conclude with a quiz
Upon completion, the next level will unlock automatically
